Frequently Asked Questions

Official answers to common questions about Jetcity, citizenship, and our micronation.

About Jetcity

What is Jetcity?
Jetcity is an officially recognized micronation founded to represent a sovereign community with its own laws, national symbols, and government services. We provide a platform for citizens to engage in civic participation, economic innovation, and international outreach.
When was Jetcity founded?
Jetcity was founded in 2025 and operates with a clear separation of executive, legislative, and judicial authority to ensure accountability and balance.
What is the capital of Jetcity?
The capital of Jetcity is Aerohaven, serving as the administrative and cultural center of our micronation.
What is the official language of Jetcity?
The official language of Jetcity is Jetzon, a constructed language developed specifically for our micronation. Resources for learning Jetzon are available on our language page.

Citizenship and Services

How do I become a citizen of Jetcity?
Citizenship is granted through our official registration process. You must provide a valid email address, create a secure password, and agree to our terms of service. Once registered, you'll receive a unique citizen ID and access to all government services.
What services are available to citizens?
Citizens have access to personalized dashboards, official documents, language learning resources, and participation in government initiatives. We also provide secure communication channels and economic opportunities within our community.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure authentication methods. Your data is protected and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. All communications are conducted through secure channels.
What is the currency of Jetcity?
The official currency of Jetcity is the JCH (Jetcity Currency Unit), used for transactions within our economic system and community initiatives.

Taxes and Finance

What is Jetcity's tax system like?
Jetcity operates a simple, transparent tax system with low rates designed to fund essential services. We use progressive income tax (5-15%), flat business tax (8%), property tax (2%), and import/export tax (3%). Our system prioritizes fairness and community benefit over complexity.
How often do I need to pay taxes?
Taxes are due annually on the anniversary of your citizenship registration. You will receive automated reminders 30 days before the due date through your citizen portal.
How are my tax dollars used?
We maintain complete transparency: 45% goes to education and culture, 25% to infrastructure, 20% to public services, and 10% to economic development. Every JCH collected directly improves our micronation.
Are there any tax exemptions or credits?
Yes, we offer several incentives: 50% credit for Jetzon education contributions, first-year business exemptions, community service reductions, environmental credits, and automatic low-income exemptions.
How do I pay my taxes?
You can pay through your citizen portal using JCH or approved cryptocurrencies, via bank transfer with your citizen ID, or set up automated monthly deductions. All methods are secure and receipt-confirmed.
What happens if I don't pay my taxes?
We prioritize education over enforcement. If you miss a payment, you'll receive reminders and support to help you comply. In extreme cases, access to certain services may be temporarily limited, but we work with all citizens to resolve issues fairly.

Technical and Access

How do I access the citizen portal?
Access the citizen portal by clicking "Citizen Portal" on our homepage. If you're not logged in, you'll be directed to the sign-in page. After authentication, you'll be redirected to your personalized dashboard.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
If you forget your password, please contact our support team through the official channels. We can help you reset your credentials securely.
Is the website mobile-friendly?
Yes, our website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers. You can access all features from any device with a modern web browser.
How can I contact the government?
You can contact the Jetcity government through our official communication channels. For general inquiries, use the contact form on our website. For urgent matters, email our administration directly.
