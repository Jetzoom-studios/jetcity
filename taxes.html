Payment Information

When to Pay

Taxes are due annually on the anniversary of your citizenship registration. You will receive automated reminders through your citizen portal 30 days before the due date.

Payment Methods

Online Portal

Secure payment through your citizen dashboard using JCH or approved cryptocurrencies.

Automated Deduction

Optional automatic monthly deductions from your registered accounts.
