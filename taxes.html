Tax Overview

Jetcity maintains a simple, citizen-first system that funds culture, services, and growth without overburdening the economy.

How We Keep It Real

  • JCH 50,000 tax-free allowance for all citizens.
  • Additional JCH 5,000 allowance per registered dependent.
  • Business income taxed separately at a flat 8% rate.
  • Foreign income is subject to a 10% withholding charge.
  • Clear budget use: education, infrastructure, services, and growth.

Payment Information

When to Pay

Annual taxes are due on the anniversary of your official citizenship registration. Reminders are sent through the citizen portal 30 days before the due date.

Accepted Methods

Citizen Portal Balance

Fast and secure. Use your portal balance to complete payments instantly.

Bank Transfer

Send funds directly to the Jetcity Treasury account with your Citizen ID as reference.

Cryptocurrency

Accepting approved cryptocurrencies for borderless contributions to the treasury.

Tax Benefits & Exemptions

Educational Investments

50% tax credit for Jetzon language and culture education contributions.

Business Startups

First-year exemption for new businesses with under JCH 10,000 revenue.

Community Service

Tax reductions for citizens participating in approved government programs.

Environmental Projects

Credits for sustainable practices and green initiatives.

Low-Income Support

Automatic exemptions for citizens below the poverty threshold.
