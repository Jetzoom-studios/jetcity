Tax Information

Transparent and fair taxation system for Jetcity citizens.

Tax Rates

Jetcity operates a simple, progressive tax system designed to fund essential services while maintaining economic fairness. Our rates are among the lowest globally, ensuring citizens keep the majority of their earnings.

Income Tax

5-15%

Progressive rates based on annual income. First JCH 50,000 tax-free.

Business Tax

8%

Flat rate for all business activities within Jetcity.

Property Tax

2%

Annual assessment on property value.

Import/Export Tax

3%

Applied to goods crossing Jetcity borders.

How Your Taxes Are Used

Every JCH collected goes directly toward improving our micronation. We maintain complete transparency in our budget allocation.

Education & Culture

45% - Jetzon language programs, cultural preservation, and citizen education initiatives.

Infrastructure

25% - Digital infrastructure, secure communication systems, and community facilities.

Public Services

20% - Citizen portal maintenance, diplomatic relations, and administrative services.

Economic Development

10% - Business incentives, innovation grants, and economic growth programs.

Payment Information

When to Pay

Taxes are due annually on the anniversary of your citizenship registration. You will receive automated reminders through your citizen portal 30 days before the due date.

Payment Methods

Online Portal

Secure payment through your citizen dashboard using JCH or approved cryptocurrencies.

Bank Transfer

Direct transfer to Jetcity Treasury account with your citizen ID as reference.

Automated Deduction

Optional automatic monthly deductions from your registered accounts.

Tax Benefits & Exemptions

  • Educational Investments: 50% tax credit for contributions to Jetzon language education
  • Business Startups: First-year exemption for new businesses under JCH 10,000 revenue
  • Community Service: Tax reduction for citizens participating in government initiatives
  • Environmental Projects: Credits for sustainable business practices
  • Low-Income Support: Automatic exemptions for citizens below poverty threshold
