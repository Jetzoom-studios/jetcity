About Jetcity

Official information from the government of Jetcity.

Confirmed by the State

Jetcity is an officially recognized micronation, founded to represent a sovereign community with its own laws, national symbols, and government services. This website is the central portal for citizens and international visitors seeking official information.

Government Structure

The nation is organized across three branches to ensure accountability and balance.

Executive Branch

Led by the President and Vice President, the executive branch implements policy, manages administration, and represents Jetcity abroad.

Legislature

The legislative body develops laws, oversees the national budget, and advances public policy in consultation with the citizenry.

Judiciary

The judiciary safeguards constitutional rights, resolves disputes, and ensures that government actions comply with the rule of law.
